# Tracking Your Order

## How to Track
1. Log in to your BookStore account
2. Go to "My Orders" from the top navigation
3. Find your order by Order ID or date
4. Click "Track Order" to see real-time status

## Order Statuses
- **Order Placed** — Your order has been received and confirmed
- **Processing** — We are picking and packing your books
- **Shipped** — Your order is on its way
- **Out for Delivery** — Your order will arrive today
- **Delivered** — Your order has been delivered

## Estimated Delivery Times
- Metro cities: 2-3 business days
- Tier 2 cities: 3-5 business days
- Remote locations: 5-7 business days

## Contact Support
If your order is delayed beyond the estimated date, contact support with your Order ID.
