# How to Place an Order

## Browsing Books
Use the search bar on the homepage to find books by title, author, or genre.
Click on any book to view details including price, availability, and description.

## Adding to Cart
Click "Add to Cart" on the book detail page.
You can add multiple books before checking out.
View your cart at any time using the cart icon in the top navigation.

## Checkout Process
1. Go to your cart and review items
2. Click "Proceed to Checkout"
3. Enter your delivery address
4. Select payment method (Credit Card, Debit Card, Net Banking, UPI)
5. Review your order summary
6. Click "Place Order" to confirm

## Order Confirmation
You will receive an email confirmation with your Order ID.
Use this Order ID to track your order status.
