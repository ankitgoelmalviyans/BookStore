# Payment Methods

## Accepted Payment Methods
BookStore accepts the following payment methods:

### Credit and Debit Cards
- Visa
- Mastercard
- American Express
- RuPay

### Net Banking
Available for all major Indian banks including SBI, HDFC, ICICI, Axis, Kotak.

### UPI
Pay using any UPI app — Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM.

### BookStore Wallet
Load money into your BookStore wallet for faster checkout.
Wallet balance never expires.

## Payment Security
All transactions are secured with 256-bit SSL encryption.
We do not store your card details on our servers.
All payments are PCI-DSS compliant.

## Payment Failure
If your payment fails:
1. Check your bank account to confirm no deduction
2. Wait 10 minutes before retrying
3. If amount was deducted but order not placed, contact support — refund within 3-5 days
