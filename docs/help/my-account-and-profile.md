# My Account and Profile

## Creating an Account
1. Click "Sign Up" on the homepage
2. Enter your name, email, and password
3. Verify your email address
4. Your account is ready

## Updating Profile
Go to "My Profile" from the top navigation to update:
- Name and contact number
- Delivery addresses (save multiple addresses)
- Email preferences
- Password

## Saved Addresses
You can save up to 5 delivery addresses.
Set a default address for faster checkout.

## Order History
View all past orders under "My Orders".
Download invoices for any order.

## Wishlist
Save books you want to buy later using the heart icon on any book.
Access your wishlist from "My Wishlist" in the navigation.

## Account Security
- Use a strong password with at least 8 characters
- Enable two-factor authentication for extra security
- Log out from shared devices after use

## Forgot Password
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page.
Enter your registered email to receive a reset link.
Reset link is valid for 24 hours.
